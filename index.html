HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID 19

~Made with love by ProGrad Junior ❤ Sachi

WASH YOUR HANDS REGULARLY

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for 30
seconds, will help you avoid germs.

COVER YOUR MOUTH AND NOSE

Cover your mouth while sneezing or coughing
in public. Even better, use a mask.

STAY HOME,STAY SAFE

Avoid travelling or being in crowded places.
Prefer staying indoors.

EAT HEALTHY

Avoid consuming junk food. Eat as healthy
as possible to boost your immunity.

STAY CALM

Regular exercise or meditation is a great way to
keep yourself calm and composed.

BE AWARE. BE SAFE
